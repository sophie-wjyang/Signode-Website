Angleboard laminated paperboard corner protectors provide sustainable product and pallet protection for a variety of markets and applications.



Angleboard

Product description: These edge protectors serve a variety of packaging functions including edge protection, pallet guarding, unitization, load stability, beam strength, and stacking strength. Angleboard is made from 100% recycled materials and is 100% recyclable.

Additional Options

Mitered Angleboard is an additional process to standard Angleboard. Mitered Angleboard is made of multiple plies of recycled paper, laminated together then formed into a rigid right angle. Notches allow the board to be easily folded in a 90 degree angle to protect the perimeter of a load. It can be used in unitization and edge protection applications. Contact your customer service representative to request this additional option.
