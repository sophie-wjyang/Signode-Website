Highly efficient rotating arm wrapping machine with a motorized power pre-stretch mechanism PPS (Power Pre-Stretch).



Cobra G Semi-automatic Wrapping Machine

Product description: Especially suitable for wrapping of light and unstable pallets up to 2,9 m height. The mechanical design of Cobra G allows different ways of fixation : to the wall, to the floor and to a pillar as well as different maximum wrapping heights and wrapping diameters. The pre-stretch level up to 300% (230% serial) gives the clients the possibility to achieve significant savings of film at continuous film tension level between machine and pallet.





Features

  • Robust and reliable mechanical structure

  • Easy to install, operate and maintain

  • Suitable for light and unstable pallets, pallets are stationary while being wrapped
