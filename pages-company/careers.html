ABOUT THE COMPANY

Wirecomm Systems (2008) Inc., a UniTek Global Services Company, provides, cable installation, maintenance, splicing, sweep, certification, balancing, disconnect and customer service to the evolving cable television and Phone industry in Canada. Other services include system activation and rebuilds, service and maintenance, MDU services, underground drop burials, ATX and other security Camera services as well as Home Theatre and cellular service installations.

ABOUT THE ROLE

We are looking for candidates who are interested in being trained to work in a Hybrid Fibre, Coaxial and Telephony network. Wirecomm is the fastest growing Installation and Service contractor in the GTA and we are looking for motivated individuals who want to start a career in this advanced field. If you do not have Industry related experience, but feel that this role will best suit your character and you are a fast learner feel free to apply.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Service and trouble-shoot cable/phone products
  • Detect and repair ingress/egress faults inside and outside a customer’s home
  • Ensure all troubleshooting work is done according to quality standards
  • Follow all company policies and procedures
  • Adhere to safety policy

RESPONSIBILITIES
